Tip Easy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Overview

Tip Easy is designed to calculate tips, scan receipts, and optionally save dining history on your device. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, or location.

Information Stored On Your Device

If you choose to save tip history or custom presets, Tip Easy stores those items locally on your device. This may include bill amounts, tip amounts, total amounts, restaurant names you enter or scan, and app preferences such as appearance and onboarding status.

You can delete local app data from the app's privacy and data settings.

Camera And Receipt Scanning

Tip Easy may request camera access so you can scan receipts. Receipt scanning is used to read receipt text, identify totals, and detect possible included service charges or gratuity. Receipt text is processed for app functionality and is not used to create an account profile.

Apple Intelligence

On supported devices and operating systems, Tip Easy may use Apple's on-device language model features to refine receipt details. This processing is intended to improve receipt parsing inside the app.

Advertising

Tip Easy may display ads through Google Mobile Ads. Google and its partners may process data such as device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, or approximate information needed to deliver, measure, and improve ads, depending on your device settings, consent choices, and applicable law.

For more information about Google's advertising practices, visit Google's advertising policy page.

Analytics And Diagnostics

The current app code does not require a login or send your saved tip history to a Tip Easy account service. Third-party advertising SDKs may collect diagnostics or usage information as described above.
