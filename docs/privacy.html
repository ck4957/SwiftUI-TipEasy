Scan Tip

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Scan Tip is designed to calculate tips, scan receipts, and optionally save dining history on your device. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, or contacts.

Information Stored On Your Device

If you choose to save tip history or custom presets, Scan Tip stores those items locally on your device. This may include bill amounts, tip amounts, total amounts, restaurant names you enter or scan, saved receipt images, approximate location details for saved tips when permission is granted, and app preferences such as appearance and onboarding status.

You can delete local app data from the app's privacy and data settings.

Location

Scan Tip may request location access while the app is in use. If you grant permission, location is used to attach a place name and map preview to saved tip history, including tips saved after scanning a receipt. Location details are stored locally on your device with the saved history item.

Camera And Receipt Scanning

Scan Tip may request camera access so you can scan receipts. Receipt scanning is used to read receipt text, identify totals, and detect possible included service charges or gratuity. Receipt text is processed for app functionality and is not used to create an account profile.

Purchases

Scan Tip Pro purchases and restores are handled by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. The app uses verified purchase status to unlock Pro features and stores the unlock state locally on your device.

Apple Intelligence

On supported devices and operating systems, Scan Tip may use Apple's on-device language model features to refine receipt details. This processing is intended to improve receipt parsing inside the app.

Advertising

Scan Tip does not include an advertising SDK in the current release.

Analytics And Diagnostics

The current app code does not require a login or send your saved tip history to a Scan Tip account service.
